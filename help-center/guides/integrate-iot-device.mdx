## Overview

Connect your IoT hardware to Aagnia's SaaS portal for real-time monitoring.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register Device">
    Log in to `https://portal.aagnia.com`, go to IoT Devices, and click "Register".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Device Token">
    Copy the unique token generated for your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Device">
    Use this sample code:
    ````python
    import requests
    requests.post('https://api.aagnia.com/iot', json={'token': 'YOUR_TOKEN', 'data': {...}})
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Connection">
    Check the portal dashboard for live device data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
