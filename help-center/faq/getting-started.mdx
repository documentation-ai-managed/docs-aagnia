## Step 1: Sign Up

Go to `https://portal.aagnia.com` and create an account using your email address.

## Step 2: Select a Service

After logging in, browse available solutions like Custom Software, IoT, or AI consulting from the dashboard.

## Step 3: Submit a Request

Click "New Project" and provide your requirements. Our team will contact you within 1 business day.
